Our services

Everything pool-leak related — from finding the leak to fixing it for good.

Leak detection

Water loss can come from dozens of places — the shell, the plumbing, the skimmer, the lights. We pinpoint the exact source instead of guessing.

  • Underground pinpoint sonar
  • Geophone acoustic listening
  • Dye testing
  • Full equipment & structure inspection

Professional diving

Our specialist dives your pool to inspect it underwater — no need to drain it, no waiting days for the water level to drop.

  • Certified underwater inspection
  • Shell & tile crack detection
  • Main drain & light niche checks
  • No draining required

Pressure testing

When the leak is in the plumbing, we test each line under pressure to identify exactly which pipe is failing.

  • Line-by-line pressure testing
  • Suction & return line diagnosis
  • Identifies hidden underground leaks
  • Clear, documented results

Repairs & plumbing

Once the leak is found, we fix it right — with quality parts and honest pricing. No upsell games.

  • Skimmer replacement
  • Plumbing line repair
  • Structural & shell repairs
  • Equipment installation
